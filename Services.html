About Our Services

We provide services to commerical, Residential, construction after clean-up, and private Communities.

Cleans rooms, including dust mopping, damp mopping, sweeping, vacuuming, dusting, picking up large objects off the floor, and spot cleaning glass and windows.

Cleans restrooms, including restocking dispensers, emptying trash, cleaning and sanitizing fixtures, cleaning mirrors, spot cleaning partition doors and walls, sweeping and mopping tile floors, and cleaning toilets and urinals.

Uses bleach and other disinfectants to disinfect and sanitize rooms and restrooms.

Empties trashcans and replaces liners

Sets up, stocks, and maintains cleaning equipment and supplies.

Transports dirty linens to the laundry room.

Restocks rooms with clean linens.

Adheres to sanitation guidelines.
